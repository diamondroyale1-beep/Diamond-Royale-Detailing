Our Story

Born from
Ambition

Diamond Royale Detailing wasn't built in a boardroom — it was built on drive. Founded in 2025 by Anthony, a self-starter with a passion for side hustles and a hunger for something more, the business grew from a simple belief: that every car deserves to be treated like a showpiece.

What started as a passion became a profession. Anthony took that entrepreneurial spirit and channelled it into a premium detailing service that refuses to cut corners — delivering results that speak louder than any sales pitch.
